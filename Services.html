Chiropractic Care

We focus on extremity care that meets your individual needs. This service offers comprehensive, non-invasive solutions for any discomfort you experience day-to-day.

Nutrition Therapy & Consultation

We believe that food is fuel. Whether you suffer from food sensitivities, nutrient deficiencies, or a high amount of inflammation, we will carefully and specifically review your diet and lifestyle with you to help you live your life to the fullest. Get started today by contacting us and scheduling your consultation.

Laser Therapy

We use low-level laser therapy to stimulate a response in the body. These lasers produce no heat, sound, or vibration. They only use light to stimulate healing, decrease pain, and control inflammation. Laser may be used to supplement the Chiropractic adjustment to assist healing and speed recovery. 

DOT Physicals

We now offer physicals that may be required for employment. This includes requirements for the Department of Transportation, pre-employment physicals, and sports physicals for schools and other organizations. We usually recommend scheduling an appointment for our services, but we welcome walk-in visits for physicals.
